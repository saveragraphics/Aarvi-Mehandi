About The Artist

Creating Beautiful
Mehendi Memories

Aarvi Mehandi Arts was born from a deep love for the ancient Indian art of henna. Based in Awadhpuri, Bhopal, we have spent over 8 years transforming brides' hands into personalized canvases of beauty and tradition.

Every design begins with a conversation — about your wedding theme, your outfit colours, your favourite Indian motifs. No two brides ever receive the same pattern.
